KPI Rules — How Fulfilment is Measured

1
Ready to Ship — 2 working days
From the moment an order is received at Olympic Paints, it must be picked, paid-confirmed, manufactured if needed, and ready for dispatch within 2 working days. Measured as date_created → date_completed.
2
Local Delivery (GP) — 5 working days
For Gauteng orders, total order-to-delivery must not exceed 5 working days. This is 2 working days dispatch + 3 working days transit estimate.
3
Regional Delivery (non-GP) — 7 working days
For all other provinces (NW, WC, NC, MP, EC, KZN, LP), total order-to-delivery must not exceed 7 working days. That is 2 working days dispatch + 5 working days transit estimate.
Working days = Monday to Friday. Weekends are excluded; public holidays are currently counted as working days.  ·  Delivery numbers are projected, not tracked — they use the transit-day estimate above, since WooCommerce does not yet capture a delivered-date.  ·  On-time % is calculated against completed/dispatched/refunded orders only; cancelled and failed orders are excluded.  ·  Bands: ≥90% green, 70–89% amber, <70% red.
